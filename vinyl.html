Vinyl Cover Redesign



he project brief was to a redesign vinyl cover from 1980-2010 and give it a contemporary look.  I redesigned the Vinyl Cover for Part II of Kate Bush’s iconic 1985 album “The Hounds Of Love”. The second part “Ninth wave’’ forms an extravagant narrative. It talks about a girl who gets thrown overboard and is stranded in the cold ocean.
