Sunny Side Breakfast Cookbook



The project was to design a typographic booklet. I decided to make a breakfast cookbook targetted at young adults who have some experience in cooking. For design, I took inspiration from ‘American Diners’ and ‘vintage cafes’. Young adults are embracing the charm of American diners and vintage cafés for their cozy, nostalgic vibes. Retro diners with jukeboxes, neon lights, and comfort food like burgers and milkshakes feel like a step back in time. Social media plays a key role, as these vibrant settings make for perfect photo opportunities.
