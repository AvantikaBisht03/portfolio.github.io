Parachute Jasmine Rebranding



Parachute is a leading hair care brand which began its journey in 1992, introducing pure coconut oil to millions. In 2000 it launched Jasmine hair oil which is a blend of 3 super potent ingredients - Coconut, jasmine and Vitamin E. In this project I rebranded Parachute along with designing a promotional print billboard campaign.
