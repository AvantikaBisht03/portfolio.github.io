Izumi Sake: Typographic packaging Design



Izumi is a peach flavoured Ginjo Sake. Made with 60% polished rice Ginjo sake is known to be light floral and fruity. In Izumi notes of peach and honey are introduced in order to enhance the fruity flavour. For creating the font, I took inspiration from Hiragana Alphabet. It is one of the three Japanese writing systems. Hiragana is very widely used in Japan. A basic understanding of hiragana is practically necessary just to start learning Japanese. The alphabets are rather rounded and have sharp edges. I incorporated that. I formed every alphabet in 2 strokes with gap in between.
