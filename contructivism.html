Glossary on Constructivism



This glossary is on the Russian Avabt-Grade movement constructivism. The movement began after the 1917 Russian revolution, in which artist sought to help the bolsheviks to build the new communist state.The constructivist artist used bold lines, sans-serif lettering, photomontage, geometric shapes to convey their messages. This glossary is designed keeping the principles of the movement in mind.
